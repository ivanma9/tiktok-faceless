Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

1. Overview

TikTok Faceless Video Publisher ("the App") is a personal-use automation tool. It is not a public-facing service and does not collect data from end users.

2. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share personal information from any third party. The only data handled is:

  • TikTok OAuth tokens: Used solely to authenticate the operator's own TikTok account for video publishing. Stored locally on the operator's machine.
  • Video content: Generated locally and uploaded directly to TikTok via their API. Not stored on any external server.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the TikTok Content Posting API. Data shared with TikTok is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

OAuth credentials are stored locally and never transmitted to any party other than TikTok's API endpoints over HTTPS.

5. Data Retention

No user data is retained beyond active OAuth sessions. Tokens can be revoked at any time through TikTok's account settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

8. Contact

For questions, open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
